Our Network

Replication Research is collaborating with a large network of international initiatives and organizations. Founded by the Münster Center for Open Science and the Framework for Open and Reproducible Research Training in 2025, it received funding from the University of Münster and the Landesinitiative Open Access.NRW. Our journal is hosted by the Hochschulbibliothekszentrum Cologne (HBZ) and we receive service support from the Ejournals team at the University of Münster.
