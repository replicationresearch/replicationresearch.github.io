# Team photos

Upload portrait photos here (JPG, PNG, or WebP). They appear automatically as
circles on the Editorial Team page after the next build — no other change
needed.

Naming: lowercase, hyphens, German umlauts transliterated (ö → oe).
The exact expected filename for every member is listed as `slug` in
`data/team.json`. Examples:

```
lukas-roeseler.jpg
miriam-mueller.png
flavio-azevedo.webp
```

Square images look best (they are cropped to a circle, `object-fit: cover`).
Members without a photo show an initials placeholder. Advisory Board members
are listed without photos.
